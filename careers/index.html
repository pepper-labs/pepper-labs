Our 6 stage engineering recruitment process

We are a small company, so we recruit as such. Our process is lightweight: less than 6 hours in total (+ a 3-day paid trial).

  1. Review. We review applications to screen for a variety of criteria.
  2. Intro call. A quick online chat with one of us to get to know you a little better.
  3. 3-hours task. A take-home technical task to assess coding abilities.
  4. Tech and team fit interview. A video interview with a PepperLabs engineer and a member of the leadership team, learning more about your technical and team experiences.
  5. 3-day paid trial. During a 3-day paid trial, we have the chance to work with each other on a real project. It's a great opportunity to meet the team and to look into our day-to-day operations.
  6. Offer. We make an offer to the candidate we feel is the best fit for the role.
